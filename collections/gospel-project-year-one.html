The Gospel Project: Year One

A three-year journey through the Bible's grand narrative

In Year One, we explore the foundations of God's story: from Creation through the Patriarchs, the Exodus and giving of the Law, the conquest of the Promised Land, and the rise of Israel's Kingdom. We'll see how God establishes His covenant people and prepares the way for the coming Messiah.
